Habitat

Morpho butterflies inhabit the primary forests of the Amazon and Atlantic. They also adapted to breed in a wide variety of other forested habitats â€“ for instance, the dry deciduous woodlands of Nicaragua and secondary forests. Morphos are found at altitudes between sea level and about 1,400 m (4,600 ft).

Biology

Morphos have a very distinctive, slow, bouncy flight pattern due to the wing area being enormous relative to the body size.
